🚀 Getting Started

1. Initial Account Setup

After downloading the app, register using your institutional email or Google account. Choosing your Role is the most critical step:

  • Trainee: Allows you to log cases, track your progress, and request supervisor verification.
  • Supervisor: Allows you to view, audit, and sign off on cases submitted by your juniors/trainees.
2. Completing your Clinical Profile

Go to the Profile tab to enter your Registration ID (PMDC, GMC, etc.), Hospital, and Department. This information is required for generating valid Portfolio PDFs.

📋 The Logbook Workflow

3. How do I add a new case efficiently?

Tap the + Add Case button on the home screen. MedLog Pro uses an intelligent categorization system:

  • Surgical Cases: Focuses on operative steps, duration, and findings.
  • Medical/Clinical Cases: Focuses on presentation, differential diagnosis, and management plans.
  • Research/Academic: For tracking presentations and publications.
4. Using the Template Engine

Tired of typing "Laparoscopic Appendectomy" details every time? Navigate to More > Manage Templates. Create a template for common procedures, and the app will automatically pre-fill the Operative Steps and Post-op Plan whenever you select that diagnosis in a new case.

☁️ Data & Cloud Synchronization

Important 5. How does the Syncing work?

MedLog Pro stores your data locally first to ensure it works in basements or operating theaters without Wi-Fi. To back up your data:

  1. Connect to the internet.
  2. Go to the More tab.
  3. Tap Upload Unsynced Data.

A green checkmark will appear next to cases that have been successfully backed up to the secure cloud.

6. Exporting to Excel / CSV

Need your data for a specific institutional audit? Use the Import / Export (CSV) tool. The app will generate a structured spreadsheet containing every detail of your logs, including complication rates and supervisor names.

📸 Media & Storage

Pro Feature 7. Adding Photos and Videos

You can attach clinical photos or short operative videos to any case. For patient privacy, we recommend avoiding identifiable facial features unless specific consent is documented.

Note: Free users can attach 1 photo per case. To attach high-definition videos and multiple follow-up photos, an upgrade to the Standard or Pro tier is required.

🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

8. I changed my phone. How do I get my data back?

Simply log in with the same email address on your new device. Go to More > Thorough Restore from Cloud. The app will reconstruct your local database from your latest cloud backup.

9. My Supervisor cannot see my cases.

Ensure that:

  • You have selected the correct supervisor from the dropdown during case entry.
  • You have synced your cases (Upload to Cloud). Supervisors view cases from the cloud, not your local device.
