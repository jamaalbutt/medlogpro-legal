🔑 1. Identity & Account Management

Defining Your Clinical Role

MedLog Pro is built around two distinct clinical personas:

  • Trainee / Resident: The primary logging role. Enables procedural tracking, logbook generation, and verification requests.
  • Supervisor / Consultant: The oversight role. Focuses on reviewing trainee logs, auditing departmental metrics, and providing digital sign-offs.

Tip: If you need to switch roles, navigate to More > Change User Role. Note that this action clears local unsynced drafts for security purposes.

Security Biometric Security & Privacy

Protecting sensitive clinical data is paramount. You can enable Biometric Unlock (Fingerprint or FaceID) via the More tab. This ensures that even if your device is unlocked, your logbook remains inaccessible to unauthorized users.

📋 2. The Core Logbook Module

Surgical Cases

Detailed tracking of operative duration, findings, anesthesia types (ASA score), and specific role (Lead Surgeon, Assistant, Observer).

Medical Cases

Optimized for clinical rounds. Track investigations, differential diagnoses, and management responses.

Log presentations, research papers, and workshops to maintain a complete professional record.

What is the "Clavien-Dindo" classification?

We integrate the international standard for grading surgical complications. When logging a case outcome, you can select Grades I through V to maintain an audit-ready complications register.

⚡ 3. High-Efficiency Templates

Streamlining Repetitive Documentation

Surgical documentation often involves repetitive descriptions of operative steps. The Template Management module allows you to:

  1. Pre-define "Steps Performed" and "Post-op Plans" for common diagnoses (e.g., Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy).
  2. When adding a case, selecting that diagnosis will automatically trigger a "Template Found" notification.
  3. Applying the template instantly populates the fields, which you can then customize for the specific patient.

🤝 4. Supervisor Verification Workflow

Digital Sign-off Ecosystem

The verification loop ensures institutional validity:

  • Registration: If your supervisor isn't listed, they must register their own account with the 'Supervisor' role first.
  • Selection: During case entry, select your supervisor from the cloud-synced list.
  • Sync: Once you upload your case to the cloud, it appears in your supervisor's "Pending Review" list.
  • Review: The supervisor reviews your logs and media attachments.
  • Status: Cases change from "Pending" to "Verified" (Green Badge), locked for further editing to maintain audit integrity.

🎯 5. Curriculum & Competency Tracking

Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs) Framework

This module tracks your progress against standard clinical milestones. You can choose between Surgical or Medical tracks to load relevant competencies for your training level.

  • Setup Wizard: When first opening the dashboard, use the wizard to select your track and set numeric targets for each EPA.
  • Progress Calculation: The app automatically links your logs to relevant competencies.
    • Finalized: Approved or finalized by a supervisor.
    • Pending: Submitted but awaiting review.
  • Status: Once finalized cases meet the target, the competency is marked as 'Met'.

📊 6. Analytics & Clinical Insights

Data-Driven Career Growth

Our analytics engine provides deep insights based on your role:

  • Residents: View operative competency, index procedure breakdowns, and monthly trends.
  • Supervisors: Monitor pending reviews, trainee counts, and overall approval rates.
  • AI Interpretation: Tap any chart to flip it and see an AI-generated interpretation of your data.
  • Advanced Metrics: Includes Index Procedures (high-stakes surgery progress) and Tenure Analysis (case volume within specific rotations).

⏱️ 7. CME Credit Hours

Automated Credit Calculation

CME hours are automatically tallied from your approved professional activities:

  • Academic Activity: Presentations and Research count for 1.0 Credit Hour each.
  • Course & Certification: Workshops and Training count for 5.0 Credit Hours each.

Note: Only 'Approved' or 'Finalized' entries are counted towards your CME total.

☁️ 9. Cloud Architecture & Data Sovereignty

Sync Engine Offline-First Design

MedLog Pro uses a Local-First architecture. Your logs are stored in a secure encrypted SQLite database (Room DB) on your phone. You control when to sync to the cloud, ensuring privacy during sensitive operations.

Cloud Recovery

If you lose your phone, our Thorough Restore feature pulls your entire history, including custom templates and media links, back onto your new device in seconds.
