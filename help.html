MedLog Pro

Help & Support Center

Guides, troubleshooting, and documentation for MedLog Pro

1. Getting Started

MedLog Pro is designed for medical trainees, residents, and consultants to document clinical cases and maintain a digital logbook. After installing the app:

  • Sign in using Google or email/password.
  • Select your role (trainee, resident, consultant, supervisor).
  • Begin adding patient cases from the dashboard.

2. Adding a New Case

To add a clinical case:

  • Tap the Add Case button.
  • Enter patient demographics (avoid unnecessary identifiers).
  • Record diagnosis, procedures, and clinical notes.
  • Upload investigation images or reports (with consent).
  • Save the case to your logbook.

3. Supervisor Review & Approval

Supervisors can review and approve cases submitted by trainees:

  • Trainees submit cases for approval.
  • Supervisors receive them in their review panel.
  • Cases can be approved, returned for correction, or commented on.

4. Editing or Deleting Cases

You may edit or delete cases at any time:

  • Open the case from your logbook.
  • Select Edit to update details.
  • Select Delete to permanently remove it.

Deleted cases cannot be recovered.

5. Data Security

MedLog Pro uses Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Storage for secure cloud-based data handling.

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • No data is shared with advertisers.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.

6. Troubleshooting

  • App not syncing: Check your internet connection and sign-in status.
  • Images not uploading: Ensure storage permissions are granted.
  • Case not saving: Ensure all required fields are filled.
  • Supervisor not visible: Confirm your institution’s supervisor mapping.
