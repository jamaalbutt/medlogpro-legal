Privacy Policy
MedLog Pro is a professional clinical logbook application. This policy explains how we handle data to support the training and documentation needs of healthcare providers.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Patient Information (Sensitive PHI)

Clinical Data: Diagnosis, procedures, outcomes, and operative notes.
Demographics: Age and gender.
Identifiers: Patient names or registration numbers (Anonymization strongly encouraged).
Media: Clinical images, investigation reports, or photographs uploaded by the user.
1.2 Professional User Profile Data

Full Name and Email Address.
Professional Details: Institution, Designation, Discipline/Specialty.
Registration: PMDC / Medical Registration Number and Qualifications.
Account Status: User role and Pro status.
2. How We Use the Data
Maintaining your clinical logbook and professional portfolio.
Facilitating the supervisor review/approval workflow.
Generating Portfolio PDFs and CSV exports.
Processing Pro features via Google Play.
3. Data Storage and Security
Storage: We use Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest.
Access Control: Only you and your nominated supervisor can view your clinical cases.
No Third-Party Sales: We never sell or share your data with advertisers.
4. Data Deletion
Users may permanently delete their account and all associated cloud data via the in-app settings. Once deleted, data is purged and cannot be recovered.

End User License Agreement (EULA)
1. Purpose of the Application
MedLog Pro is a clinical logbook for documenting cases, procedures, and training. It is NOT a substitute for institutional Electronic Medical Records (EMR).

2. User Responsibility for Consent
You are solely responsible for obtaining informed consent from patients (or guardians) before entering data or images. You are strongly encouraged to use initials or anonymized identifiers instead of full patient names.

3. Compliance With Institutional Policies
You are responsible for ensuring that the use of MedLog Pro is permitted within your organization.
