1. Introduction

MedLog Pro is an educational logbook and portfolio-building application designed for use by advanced trainees, residents, and supervisors. The app is intended exclusively for training documentation and tracking academic progress, and not for children or general public use.

This Privacy Policy explains how MedLog Pro collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and training log information entered by authorized users for the purpose of maintaining professional training logbooks.

By using MedLog Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

MedLog Pro collects the following categories of data only when entered by the user:

2.1 Training Log Information (User‑Generated Data)

  • Log demographics (age ranges, classifications, academic identifiers entered by the user)
  • Operational classification and procedural tracking data
  • Technical milestones, operational activities performed, and educational logs
  • Photographs of logbooks, investigation reports, or educational tracking sheets
  • Case logs and progress follow‑up notes

2.2 User Information

  • Email address used for authentication (Google Sign‑In or email/password)
  • User role (trainee, resident, student, supervisor)
  • Activity logs (entries added, edited, approved)

2.3 Device Information

  • Basic device metadata (Android version, device model)
  • Crash logs (if enabled by device settings)

MedLog Pro does NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • SMS
  • Microphone or camera data (except when manually uploading images)
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use the Data

The data collected is used solely for professional training and educational documentation, including:

  • Maintaining a digital logbook of educational activities and entries
  • Tracking trainee milestones and performance growth
  • Allowing supervisors to review and verify logged hours
  • Supporting academic curriculum and institutional training requirements
  • Secure storage and retrieval of personal training portfolios

No data is used for advertising, marketing, or commercial profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed under:

  • Legitimate professional and educational use
  • User consent when documenting training records
  • Milestone requirements of affiliated educational institutions

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that all data entered consists strictly of de‑identified academic records and completely complies with their local training institution’s privacy, data handling, and compliance policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

MedLog Pro uses Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage for secure cloud storage.

Security Measures

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Role‑based access control
  • Authentication required for all access
  • No public access to user log data
  • Audit logs maintained for database updates

Firebase Services Used

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

Firebase Analytics is NOT used. No advertising SDKs are integrated.

6. Data Sharing

MedLog Pro does not share user data with third parties except:

  • Firebase (cloud service provider)
  • Authorized supervisors or program coordinators within the workflow

We do not sell, rent, or disclose data to advertisers or external organizations.

7. Data Retention & Account Deletion Policy

We preserve your data only as long as your account remains active. MedLog Pro respects user autonomy over their information and provides comprehensive deletion options:

  • In-App Self-Service: Users can manually delete single operational log entries directly within the log view. Deletion of individual records is processed immediately.
  • Complete Account Purge: Users may terminate their profile and completely purge all associated databases, logs, professional files, and media instances by navigating to the "Profile" Tab inside the application and selecting "Delete Account & All Data".
  • Web/Email Deletion Track: If you have already removed the app from your device, you can trigger data deletion through our web tracking procedures. For complete steps, visit our dedicated Account & Data Deletion Page. Alternatively, contact us directly at jamaalbutt@gmail.com. Manual verification requests are completely fulfilled within 7 business days.

8. User Rights

In accordance with global mobile development data frameworks, users retain distinct rights over their telemetry and workspace datasets:

  • The right to request immediate account termination.
  • The right to review, edit, or clear individual operational files.
  • The right to check or update profile registry parameters.
  • The right to request manual data extraction or correction reports via email.

9. Children’s Privacy

MedLog Pro is not intended for children and should only be used by advanced trainees, professional students, and adult educational users. The app does not target or collect data from minors as users.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The “Last Updated” date will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, contact:
Jamaal Butt
Email: jamaalbutt@gmail.com
