1. Introduction

MedLog Pro is a clinical logbook application designed for use by medical professionals, including trainees, residents, and consultants. The app is intended exclusively for healthcare providers and not for children or general public use.

This Privacy Policy explains how MedLog Pro collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and sensitive patient information entered by authorized users for the purpose of maintaining clinical training logbooks.

By using MedLog Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

MedLog Pro collects the following categories of data only when entered by the user:

2.1 Patient Information (Sensitive Data)

  • Patient demographics (age, gender, identifiers entered by the user)
  • Clinical diagnosis and disease information
  • Treatment details, procedures performed, and clinical notes
  • Photographs of investigations, reports, or clinical findings
  • Case summaries and follow‑up notes

2.2 User Information

  • Email address used for authentication (Google Sign‑In or email/password)
  • User role (trainee, resident, consultant, supervisor)
  • Activity logs (cases added, edited, approved)

2.3 Device Information

  • Basic device metadata (Android version, device model)
  • Crash logs (if enabled by device settings)

MedLog Pro does NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • SMS
  • Microphone or camera data (except when manually uploading images)
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use the Data

The data collected is used solely for clinical training and documentation, including:

  • Maintaining a digital logbook of clinical cases
  • Tracking trainee progress
  • Allowing supervisors to review and approve cases
  • Supporting academic and training requirements
  • Secure storage and retrieval of clinical documentation

No data is used for advertising, marketing, or commercial profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed under:

  • Legitimate clinical and educational use
  • User consent when entering patient data
  • Training requirements of affiliated institutions

Users are responsible for ensuring that patient data is entered in compliance with their hospital’s privacy and confidentiality policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

MedLog Pro uses Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage for secure cloud storage.

Security Measures

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Role‑based access control
  • Authentication required for all access
  • No public access to patient data
  • Audit logs maintained for case activity

Firebase Services Used

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

Firebase Analytics is NOT used. No advertising SDKs are integrated.

6. Data Sharing

MedLog Pro does not share patient data with third parties except:

  • Firebase (cloud service provider)
  • Authorized supervisors or consultants within the workflow

We do not sell, rent, or disclose data to advertisers or external organizations.

7. Data Retention

  • Data is retained as long as the user account remains active.
  • Users may delete individual cases at any time.
  • Account deletion permanently removes all associated data.

8. User Rights

Users may:

  • Request account deletion
  • Request deletion of specific cases
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Export their logbook data (if implemented)

Requests can be made via email: jamaalbutt@gmail.com

9. Children’s Privacy

MedLog Pro is not intended for children and should only be used by licensed healthcare professionals. The app does not target or collect data from minors as users.
